RECITALS

The parties are currently co-tenants under a residential lease agreement (“Lease”) for the above-referenced property. The parties mutually desire to remove Jamie Young from the Lease, with Isaac Breitenbach continuing as sole tenant.

AGREEMENT

1. REMOVAL FROM LEASE. Effective on the date this Agreement is fully executed by both parties and acknowledged by the Landlord, Jamie Young shall be removed from the Lease as a tenant.

2. RELEASE OF LIABILITY. Upon Landlord’s written acknowledgment of this Agreement, Jamie Young shall be released from all future obligations arising under the Lease, including but not limited to rent payments, utility charges, and property damage occurring after the effective date.

3. ASSUMPTION OF OBLIGATIONS. Isaac Breitenbach hereby assumes sole responsibility for all obligations under the Lease from the effective date forward, including monthly rent, utilities, and maintenance of the premises.

4. SECURITY DEPOSIT. The security deposit was paid solely by Isaac Breitenbach. Jamie Young acknowledges that she has no claim to any portion of the security deposit, and the full deposit shall remain applied to the Lease for the benefit of Isaac Breitenbach as the sole remaining tenant.

5. KEYS AND ACCESS. Jamie Young shall return all keys, access cards, garage openers, and any other means of entry to the property to Isaac Breitenbach on or before: . Upon execution of this Agreement, Jamie Young shall have no right of entry to the property for any reason. Any entry without the express written consent of Isaac Breitenbach shall be considered unauthorized.

6. PERSONAL PROPERTY. Jamie Young acknowledges and agrees that she has already retrieved all personal belongings from the property prior to the execution of this Agreement. Jamie Young waives any and all claims to any items remaining at the property and agrees that no further access to the property shall be granted for the purpose of retrieving belongings.

7. NO FURTHER CLAIMS. Jamie Young agrees that upon execution of this Agreement, she relinquishes any and all claims of residency, tenancy, or occupancy at the property. Jamie Young shall not represent to any person, entity, or authority that she resides at or has any right to access the property.

8. EXISTING DAMAGES. Both parties acknowledge that as of the date of this Agreement, any existing damage to the property is the shared responsibility of both parties for the period of joint tenancy. Isaac Breitenbach assumes responsibility for the condition of the property from the effective date forward.

9. LANDLORD ACKNOWLEDGMENT. This Agreement is contingent upon written acknowledgment by the Landlord or property management company. Both parties agree to cooperate in obtaining such acknowledgment.

10. GOVERNING LAW. This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Oklahoma.

11. ENTIRE AGREEMENT. This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties regarding the removal of Jamie Young from the Lease and supersedes any prior oral or written agreements on this subject.
